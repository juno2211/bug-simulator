Hercules Beetle

The Hercules beetle (Dynastes hercules) is one of the largest beetles in the world. Known for its impressive horn, which males use in battles for mates, it can grow up to 7 inches (17 cm) long. The beetle has a shiny, hard exoskeleton with colors ranging from yellow to green, and its horn is jet black. Native to the rainforests of Central and South America, the Hercules beetle is a marvel of insect strength, capable of carrying up to 850 times its own body weight.
